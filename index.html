TEAM 11: PABLO's HONDA CIVIC SPORT 🚗

Motivation Statement 🚀

The aim of PowerPlant 🪴 is to use hedonistic design principles and the functional aspect seen in the CopenHill powerplant 🏞 and apply them to a sanitizer 🚿 to be placed in Zachry Engineering Building.

5 Week Milestone

  1. Week of 29th March: Initial planning and ordering of materials.
  2. Week of 5th April: Building the prototype
  3. Week of 12th April: Coding the prototype if necessary
  4. Week of 19th April: Testing the prototype and recording the video/time-lapse in Zachry
  5. Week of 26th April: Finalizing website, videos, photos, description of system processes and final presentation

Week of 29th March

This week, we ordered the necessary materials for the prototype. In fact, some team members have spare hardware from previous projects available, so we did not have to order everything. We also had a meeting in which we planned in more detail the features we will build. In particular, we plan to have some kind of small reward for sanitizing, such as a device which responds "thanks for doing your part" when used or a nice animation. We also planned the logistics of when to meet and work on the following weeks' milestones.
