Week of 19th April

For most of this week, we worked on putting together the film and animations that will be used in our final presentation video. In particular, we brought PowerPlant to Zachry in order to demonstrate what it would look like in our target space. There, we hooked up PowerPlant to a monitor that would give passersby an indication of how to interact with the plant, as well as a QR code to scan if they wanted to share their usage of PowerPlant with their friends on social media.

5 Week Milestone

  1. Week of 29th March: Initial planning and ordering of materials.
  2. Week of 5th April: Building the prototype
  3. Week of 12th April: Coding the prototype if necessary
  4. Week of 19th April: Testing the prototype and recording the video/time-lapse in Zachry
  5. Week of 26th April: Finalizing website, videos, photos, description of system processes and final presentation
